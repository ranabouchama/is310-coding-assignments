The Food Timeline

The Food Timeline began as a series of books chronicling the history of food, and specifically recipes. Beginning from 17,000 BC and ending with the modern day (specifically 2013). It was later digitized by its author Lynne Oliver before being bought by Virginia Tech

Relevant information

  • No Github repository
  • Both born digital and digitized

Other

Important source on the history of cooking, although it doesn't give dates for early ingredients (since that isn't possible) it still provides a lot of interesting information regarding early civilizations and what they were cooking.
